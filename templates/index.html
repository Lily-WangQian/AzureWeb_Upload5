The Tool

Sustainable finance reporting in the financial industry is often heterogeneous and inconsistent. Key themes such as financed emissions are frequently omitted or reported in non-standardized ways. As sustainability regulations and standards continue to expand globally, the need for analytical tools that assess alignment between reports and formal frameworks has become increasingly important.

The Sustainability Report Alignment (SRA) tool addresses this challenge by analyzing similarities, differences, and thematic alignment between financial institutions’ sustainability reports and established sustainability standards and regulations.

By applying advanced natural language processing techniques, SRA enables users to identify areas of convergence and divergence between reporting practices and regulatory expectations.

How does it work?

  1. Click Upload Report (.pdf) and choose the PDF document you want to analyze.
  2. Select a standard/regulation from the dropdown menu.
  3. Click Calculate. The analysis will start.
  4. A results report will be displayed as soon as the analysis is finalized.

The list of standards and regulations can be viewed on the Standards page.
